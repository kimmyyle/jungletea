About Jungle Tea
Jungle Tea of Mira Mesa in San Diego county is the first of its kind. We are family owned and operated locally. We serve many different types of drinks, from milk tea to fruit teas, and smoothies to specialties, all served with the best quality.
Our quality and freshness
Our internal research and development team produces the highest quality drinks that serves our customers daily. In addition, our high-quality tea and products arrive from Taiwan. Having delicious drinks while creating a modern jungle atmosphere with friendly customer service is our main goal.
Our drinks and toppings
You can expect to be amazed when you try our drinks. Boba is known as "the drink you can eat". Our premium tea leaves, bursting boba, boba, and jellies are made from only the freshest ingredients sourced from Taiwan. Our fruit juices are even made from real fruit juice. Come on in and try all of our drinks. You won't be disappointed.
